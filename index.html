About Us


  • AIM

    UDYOGAM, the Gauhati University initiative for providing a common platform to synergize industry-academia efforts to come up with an Outcome Based Education model for technical education, aims at empowering the youth from the North-Eastern part of the country to achieve a sustainable ecosystem through science and technology, enabling holistic development of the human society through inclusive socio-economic growth.

  • VISION

    To bring about holistic development of the human society through timely intervention of science and technology, mobilizing industrial collaboration in academia.
